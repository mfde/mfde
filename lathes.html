Lathes

About our lathes

What is a lathe?

A lathe is a machine which rotates a work material on an axis, allowing for radial operations to be performed. Many circular objects and shapes can be worked with a lathe, and precise operations such as facing, turning, drilling and cutting can be performed.

In contrast to a milling machine, the tools of a lathe are typically fixed and the work material is rotating.

How we use them

The lathe forms a primary part of our machining side at MF Delic Engineering, to turn rough materials into smooth and precise components.

Tip #1

Different materials require different settings to produce clean and accurate results. For example, it is necessary to adjust the speed of rotation on a lathe for different types of steel, to avoid heat-expansion issues which can affect precision, and reduce the wearing-down of tips.

Tip #2
