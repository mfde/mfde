Overview

MF Delic Engineering is a local metal-working business based in Sydney, Australia operating since 1989

Who are we?

MF Delic Engineering started out of a small garage in 1989, with 20 years prior experience. The founder and expert behind it all is Muriz Delic, who loves working in the field.

We have since moved into a larger factory in Chipping Norton, undertaking larger projects over the past three decades.

Skills

Over the years we have further developed our skills and expanded our services, allowing us to do all types of metal work including:

  • Precision Engineering

  • Metal Fabrication

  • Precision Tooling

  • Machining

  • Precision Tooling

  • Prototyping

  • Balustrading

  • Custom Metal Work

Experience

We have over 20 years of experience, with licenses in Precision Engineering and Balustrading and have worked with a number of private commercial and government organisations, as well as individuals looking for residential upgrades.
