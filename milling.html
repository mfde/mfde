Milling Machine

About our milling machine

What is a milling machine?

A milling machine uses rotary tools to perform operations on multiple axis, including tasks such as drilling, facing. In contrast to a lathe where the work material is being rotated, the milling machine tool is rotating and the work material is fixed.

Due to the set up of a milling machine, it allows for extremely precise planar operations to be performed, including facing, drilling and general deformation.

How we use it

The milling machine is used regularly at MF Delic Engineering to cut clean lines, holes and shapes into components.
