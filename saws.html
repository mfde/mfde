About our saws

How we use our saws

Every job starts with lengths of material that are the wrong size or shape, as material is often bought in standard lengths to reduce cost. The saw helps to reduce the size of those lengths to where more precise work can be done with minimal material waste.

Our saws can cut through steel, aluminium, brass, plastic, etc !

Cutting material is one part of the fabrication process that we do at MF Delic Engineering.
